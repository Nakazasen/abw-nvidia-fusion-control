# PROJECT_PHILOSOPHY

## Purpose

Capture the governing philosophy that constrains all roadmap and implementation interpretation.

## Ownership

- Canonicality: Canonical philosophy document

## SOURCE OF TRUTH

## Philosophy

- Local-first truth over remote assumption.
- Evidence over narrative.
- Bounded confidence over optimistic certainty.
- Safety and auditability over speed theater.
- Explicit non-claims over implied capability inflation.

## Bounded-Readiness Doctrine

- A pass in bounded/synthetic scope is still bounded.
- Passing tests do not automatically imply production readiness.
- Readiness is a governance decision backed by convergent evidence, not a single milestone.

## Historical Lineage Doctrine

- Fail artifacts are first-class truth.
- Rerun passes do not erase prior fails.
- Chronology must be preserved as operational memory.
